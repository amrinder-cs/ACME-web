Logo Designing

ArenaX - Digital Identity Challenge

ArenaX represents the excitement and energy of competitive gaming. It’s a place where players compete and enjoy the thrill of gaming and reach new heights in the world of eSports. Your task is to create a logo that shows the energy and excitement of eSports and the spirit of working together as a team.

Requirements:

  • Colour Palette:
    #202040 (Dark Indigo)
    #FF4C4C (Vibrant Red)
    #FFD700 (Bright Yellow)
    #39FF14 (Neon Green)
    #7DF9FF (Electric Blue)
  • Logo Elements:
    • Dynamic Arena Symbol: Create a cool and stylish design for the word "Arena" that looks like an exciting gaming battlefield.
    • Colours Effects: Use bright and bold colours like electric blue, neon green, or bright orange with glowing effects to give it a high-energy, digital look.
    • Typography: Pick a modern and bold font like Racing Sans One or Impact, with sharp edges that look fast and powerful.
    • Iconography: Add gaming symbols like controllers, headsets, or trophies to make it feel connected to the gaming world.
    • Pixel Art Influence: Add a touch of old-school games with pixelated designs, appealing to gamers who love both classic and new games.
    • Tagline Integration: Add a short phrase like “Unleash Your Potential” or “Empower Your Play” to capture the spirit of ArenaX. This is optional but can make it even better.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Permitted Design Tools: Google Slides, Canva, Adobe Photoshop, Corel Draw, MS Office, Paint.
  • Size: 800 x 800 pixels
  • Submission Process: A drive link will be provided at the end, upload your entries there only.

EduNexus - Digital Identity Challenge

EduNexus is an online learning platform that connects students, teachers, and resources in one place. It helps students learn beyond their classrooms and join a global community of learners, making education easier, more exciting, and available to everyone. Your task is to create a logo that reflects the passion for education and the importance of community in the learning journey.

Requirements:

  • Colour Palette:
    Blue: #007BFF
    Orange: #FF8C00
    Turquoise: #40E0D0
    Electric Purple: #A020F0
    Mustard Color: #FFD700
  • Logo Elements:
    • Symbolic Icon: Use an icon with the letter ‘E’ from "EduNexus" or the word “EduNexus” that represents learning and connection.
    • Typography: For ease of reading, use a clean, modern sans-serif font (such as Montserrat or Righteous).
    • Iconography: Add symbols like a book, graduation cap, or light bulb to show that it’s about education.
    • Tagline Integration: You can include a short phrase like “Learn and Grow” or “Connect and Succeed” to make it more inspiring.
    • Gradients and Depth: Use gradient effects to add depth and dimension to the logo, making it more engaging.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Permitted Design Tools: Google Slides, Canva, Adobe Photoshop, Corel Draw, MS Office, Paint.
  • Size: 800 x 800 pixels
  • Submission Process: A drive link will be provided at the end, upload your entries there only.
